# DreamPlay Shop Work — Next Logical Steps

## Goal
Use real DreamPlay products and media to turn the concept-clone site into the actual DreamPlay Pianos shop destination, starting with the highest-impact pages.

## Priority decision
Top-priority pages:
1. Homepage
2. Shop page

Recommended order:
- Work on one page first: Homepage
- Then move immediately to Shop

Reason:
- The homepage is the brand-to-shop handoff page. It sets the visual language, product hierarchy, CTA direction, and trust layer for the entire storefront.
- Once the homepage uses the right DreamPlay products, assets, and messaging, the shop page can inherit a more grounded product narrative.

## Recommended immediate execution plan

### Phase 1 — Homepage product/story mapping
Objective:
- Decide what the homepage is actually selling and in what order.

Deliverables:
- a homepage merchandising map
- a shortlist of real DreamPlay assets for each homepage section
- a list of placeholder sections to replace or rewrite

Questions to resolve in this phase:
- What is the flagship piano?
- Are we leading with one hero model or a family of models?
- Do we want the homepage to emphasize:
  - ergonomic key sizing
  - premium design
  - family learning
  - app ecosystem
  - bundles/accessories
  - founder credibility

### Phase 2 — Homepage first pass
Objective:
- Replace the most important fake/generic visuals and messaging on the homepage with real DreamPlay product and brand logic.

Scope for first homepage pass:
- hero section
- first product/story section
- one supporting lifestyle section
- one app/connectivity section
- one premium/editorial section
- CTA paths pointing toward the real shop flow

Success criteria:
- homepage clearly feels like DreamPlay Pianos, not a generic theme clone
- strongest real assets are visible above the fold and in the first key sections
- CTA language aligns with the future storefront role

### Phase 3 — Shop page product structure mapping
Objective:
- Turn `/shop` into a cleaner representation of actual DreamPlay merchandising priorities.

Questions to resolve:
- What are the actual shop categories?
- Which models/accessories should appear first?
- Which products are hero products versus supporting/accessory products?
- What naming, pricing placeholders, and merchandising badges need revision?

### Phase 4 — Shop page first pass
Objective:
- Replace the highest-visibility shop imagery and product-card logic with real DreamPlay-oriented merchandising.

Scope for first shop pass:
- shop hero/banner imagery
- top 6–8 product cards
- filter/category framing
- supporting editorial block near lower page
